Featured Case Study

01 / DIELINE-TO-SHELF

Sunrise Foods — Snack Packaging Redesign

Personal project · Brand identity, packaging design, production art

A complete packaging concept for a snack food brand, taken from brand strategy through to a print-ready dieline. The goal: replace a cluttered, low-shelf-impact bag with a design system that reads clearly at a distance, differentiates by flavor, and holds up across a full product line.

Brief

Modernize a flagging snack brand without alienating its existing buyers. Improve shelf standout across a 4-flavor range while keeping production costs flat.

Process

Audited the competitive set, defined a flavor-coded color system, sketched thumbnail layouts, then built production artwork in Illustrator — testing legibility at shelf-viewing distance before finalizing dielines.

Solution

A bold typographic mark, one dominant color per flavor, and a simplified information hierarchy that separates brand, flavor, and claims into clear reading zones.

Production

Delivered press-ready files with accurate dieline alignment, bleed, and color separations, built to spec for commercial flexographic printing.
